Women's Care - Exercise

Focused on Pregnancy, Period, and Diet Exercises

Pelvic Tilts

Strengthens your lower back and core.

Pelvic tilts help relieve lower back pain during pregnancy. Perform 3 sets of 15 repetitions.

Cat-Cow Stretch

Relieves tension in the spine.

This stretch helps improve spinal mobility and relaxes back muscles. Perform 10-15 repetitions.

Hip Bridges

Helps with menstrual cramps and strengthens the hips.

Hip bridges can relieve menstrual cramps and improve lower body strength. Do 3 sets of 12 reps.

Child's Pose

Relaxing stretch for your back and hips.

Child's pose is a calming yoga pose that reduces menstrual discomfort and relieves tension. Hold for 30 seconds.
